Quick Answers

FAQs

Do I need to book in advance?

We recommend booking at least a week ahead for colour and beauty appointments as these are our most popular slots. For cuts and quick treatments, we often have same-week availability. Walk-ins are welcome but subject to availability.

What's your cancellation policy?

We ask for at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule. This allows us to offer the slot to another client. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a charge.

Do you offer a patch test for colour?

Yes — a patch test is required at least 48 hours before any colour service if it's your first time with us, or if it's been more than 6 months since your last colour appointment. We can do these quickly in-salon or send home test kits.

Do you offer gift vouchers?

Absolutely! Gift vouchers are available in any denomination and make a perfect present. Purchase in-salon or contact us to arrange delivery. Vouchers are valid for 12 months.

Is parking available nearby?

Yes, there is [parking details to be confirmed] nearby. We're also well served by public transport — get in touch if you need directions and we'll help.
