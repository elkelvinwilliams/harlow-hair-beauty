Our Story

More than braids —
a crowning

Hagiazo was founded in the heart of Harlow on one belief: that protective styling is an art form, and every head of hair deserves to be treated as something set apart.

We specialise in braids and locs for the Afro-Caribbean community — combining authentic technique, healthy-hair care and a calm, unhurried chair that keeps our clients coming back time and again.
