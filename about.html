Our Story

The meaning behind
the name

Hagiazo (verb) — to sanctify, to make holy, to set apart for a special purpose. It's more than our name; it's our promise. Every client who sits in our chair is set apart, given our full attention and our finest craft.

Over a decade later, that belief is still at the heart of everything we do. From the moment you walk through our doors, we're focused entirely on you — your hair, your wellbeing, your confidence.

We invest in the finest products, the latest techniques, and — most importantly — in our people. Our team attends regular training and industry events to ensure that what we offer is always at the cutting edge.
