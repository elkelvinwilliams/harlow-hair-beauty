Our Story

Loving people through
their transformation

Loving people through their transformation has always been part of who I am. It led me first into therapy — walking with people through their growth and their healing — until I realised I'd been given a second gift doing the very same work: hair.

Doing hair, I watched that same transformation happen — only now it was visible. My friends and family kept testifying to it: they didn't just leave looking different, they left feeling different. That's when I received the vision to bring the two together — the therapist and the stylist, the inner work and the outer — under one roof.

Because hair is more than an appointment. It's a space to feel safe. It's intimacy and fellowship. It's the quiet confidence that rises in you when you catch yourself in the mirror — and I want to be part of that process, every time.

From the moment you enter the room, to the time you settle into the chair, to the moment you walk back out, you begin the journey of becoming set apart. The transformation is never only physical. It's a renewing of the mind.

Close to fifteen years on, that vision is Hagiazo Hair — where box braids and knotless, cornrows, locs and retwists, and bespoke wig-making and revamps meet the kind of care that renews.
